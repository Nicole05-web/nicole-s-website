ABOUT THE PROGRAM

The "feminine Warriors Internship Program was created by Ms Nicole Thandeka Moyo in 2013, she had a vision that if more girls were in the army, that would combat social stigmas that are towards jobs that are gender assigned.
Women are just as capable, with dedication and determination, regardless of what people say. Ms Moyo believed that women were not motivated enough to take up certain careers and this is why she came up with this program, to motivate and encourage women.This is a six month program including intensified military training with the support from the SANDF.
"Social stigmas are only there to bring you down, telling you what you can and cannot do, your abilities are stored deep in you, its the drive, the passion, so take your shot and remember if he can do it so can you."-Ms Nicole Moyo
